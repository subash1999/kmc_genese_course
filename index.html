Kathmandu Model College (KMC) established in 2000 right in the heart of the capital city Bagbazar has carved out for itself an enviable niche at the summit of the best colleges in the country. KMC has earned its place of price through the vision and dedication of a team of academics and professionals who have promoted it, the skill of its panel of professors who possess cutting edge knowledge in their fields, and, above all, the brilliance of its students who have been consistently turning in sterling performance every year. KMC reaffirms its determination to build on the achievement of recent excellent performance in the form of 100% success in Humanities, Science and Management. The growing number of students every year, despite our selective approach to their admission into the college, reveals our popularity and reputation. As its motto 'Academic Excellence through Quality Education' it never compromised to provide quality education. It has given high priority to select excellent subject teachers, who not only can teach but also encourage students for betterment. We have tried hard to make all programmes attractive and intellectually stimulating by inviting scholars of national and international standing for workshops and seminars. This adds one more bright feather to the already glorious cap of the college. Its past HSEB result was excellent since its establishment. It has been ranked upto fifth including first among 3500 plus higher secondary schools. It not only produced higher scores in HSEB examination but also produced quality output in competitive examination. A number of students got success to obtain scholarship in MBBS, BE, CA, Agriculture, BBA and in different national and international reputed universities has proven its quality education. No wonder,the school was established with 63 students including science 42 and management 13 and humanitites 8 together. Now this school has been academic home for about 3000 students and become a school of choice for most of the students of the country.
